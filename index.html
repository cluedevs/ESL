FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


You must be legally of age and must have a driver’s license according to the vehicle you want to rent.

We accept cash, transfers and credit cards.

The price includes the use of the chosen vehicle for the time rented, also includes an accidents insurance which covers material damages of our vehicle. It does not include medical insurance or third-party damages.

We give the vehicle with a full tank. You can fill the tank before returning the vehicle or you can pay us the necessary amount to fill the tank when you return it.

We don't have any limit for miles to use, you can drive as far as you want inside the country.

We have a tolerance of 30 minutes, after that time you will be charged for the fraction of the time that you were late
