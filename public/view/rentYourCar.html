Earn extra money with your car!

Do you have your car free several days a week? You can place your car for rent with us and earn extra money.
Car requirements:
  • Be in excellent condition.
  • Be at most eight years old and have less than 80,000 kilometers.
  • Have an active accident insurance for material damages.
  • Have legal documentation up to date.
Conditions:
  • The lease of your car will depend on the demand. We can’t guarantee there will always be clients.
  • The fee will depend on the category of your car. Currently there are four categories available: economy, family, SUV or pickup. Our share is 20%.
  • You must let us know the availability of your car at least 24 hours beforehand. You cannot make changes after this point.
  • You must leave your car with full fuel tank. You will receive it the same way.
Registering for the first time:

Please fill in the form with your data and the data from your car. In few days you will be contacted for an inspection of your car and documentation and if everything is ok, we’ll sign a contract.
